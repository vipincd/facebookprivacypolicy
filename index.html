Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13.12.2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how this app (“the App”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information depending on the Facebook permissions you grant:

  • Public Facebook profile information (name, profile photo, email)
  • Facebook Page details (if you manage a Page)
  • Content you choose to generate or post through the App
  • Technical information (device type, IP address, browser type)

The App does NOT collect or access your password or private messages.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Provide and operate the App’s features
  • Generate posts or content you request
  • Post content to your Facebook Page (only with your permission)
  • Improve performance and fix technical issues
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

We never sell, rent, or trade your data to any third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We may share your information only with:

  • Facebook (as required for login and posting)
  • Service providers (only if needed for technical operation)
  • Legal authorities, if required by law

We do not sell your data.

5. Data Storage and Security

We use standard security measures to protect your data. Your data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary for the App’s functions.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw Facebook permissions at any time
  • Stop using the App at any time

To remove permissions, visit: Facebook → Settings → Apps and Websites → Remove App

7. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13.
